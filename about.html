This is Rainy Days

Rainy Days was founded after a long trip in the Norwegian nature where the clothes did not reach expectations. We found that we wanted to research how we could present to our customers goods that can withstand the changing weather we experience here in Norway.

Everything can happen when you are on a trip here, from glorious sun in the morning, to rain with a rainbow during the day and sleet and cold in the evening. With a lot of research, we have arrived at jackets of good quality at good prices so our customers can enjoy the Norwegian nature as it is meant to be enjoyed.
